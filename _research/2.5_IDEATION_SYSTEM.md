## CÁCH 1: DAILY OBSERVATION (2-3 phút/ngày)

**Idea trigger:** Cuộc sống, con, bản thân

### **Khi nào ghi chép:**

**Morning (sau khi thức dậy):**
- Con hỏi cái gì mới hôm nay?
- Tôi cảm thấy gì?
- Có moment "aha" nào?

**Afternoon (giờ trưa/cà phê):**
- Công việc gặp tình huống gì?
- Tôi xử lý thế nào?
- Có learning nào?

**Evening (trước ngủ):**
- Hôm nay con dạy tôi gì?
- Tôi thay đổi tư duy gì?
- Moment quý nào xảy ra?

### **How to capture:**

**Method 1: Notes app (simplest)**
- Open phone, type 1-2 sentence
- Ví dụ: "Con sợ tiêm, tôi xử lý bằng cách nghe thay vì dỏi"
- Save vào folder "Content Ideas"

**Method 2: Voice memo**
- Talk to phone, transcribe later
- Easier when busy

**Method 3: Quick note in 3.2_CONTENT_IDEAS_BANK.xlsx**
- Open spreadsheet, add row
- Habit building

### **Example daily observations:**

```
Morning:
- Con tôi: "Mẹ, sao mẹ hay tức?" 
  → Idea: Cách manage cảm xúc của mẹ
  
Afternoon:
- Meeting tôi xử lý conflict bằng cách nghe team
- Tối con tôi áp dụng: "Mẹ, mình lắng nghe mẹ được không?"
  → Idea: Communication skill từ parenting to career

Evening:
- Con nhảy múa cho mẹ, tôi nhận ra: mình cần give space cho con thử
  → Idea: Parenting mà không kiểm soát
```

## CÁCH 2: AUDIENCE QUESTIONS (5 phút/tuần)

**Idea trigger:** Audience cần gì?

### **Where to find questions:**

1. **Comments trên posts:**
   - Đọc comments
   - Note cái gì audience hỏi nhiều
   - Ví dụ: "Làm sao manage nếu con hay khóc?"

2. **DM / Messages:**
   - Audience hỏi gì?
   - Recurring questions?

3. **Facebook parenting groups:**
   - Join groups → lurk
   - Xem mẹ khác hỏi cái gì
   - Có thể là idea

4. **Surveys (advanced):**
   - Ask: "What's your biggest struggle with parenting?"
   - Collect answers → ideas

### **Process:**

**Weekly (Saturday, 5 phút):**
1. Review past week comments
2. Note 2-3 most common questions
3. Add to 3.2_CONTENT_IDEAS_BANK.xlsx
4. Mark as "From audience"

### **Example:**

```
Question from comments: "How to say no to child without guilt?"
→ Idea: Setting boundaries as learning, not punishment
→ Content type: Story + Tips
→ Tuyến: 1 + 3
```

## CÁCH 3: CONTENT SWIPE (10 phút/tuần)

**Idea trigger:** Từ creators khác (learn structure, không copy content)

### **What to swipe:**

❌ **DON'T:** Copy content, copy exact story
✅ **DO:** Swipe structure, angle, hook, format

### **Content sources:**

1. **TikTok creators (parenting, self-growth):**
   - Follow 5-10 creators in niche
   - Scroll weekly (10 phút)
   - Save videos that hook you
   - Ask: "Why did this hook me?"

2. **Instagram reels / Facebook:**
   - Similar process

3. **YouTube parenting/coaching channels:**
   - Watch 1-2 videos/week
   - Note structure

4. **Substack / Medium essays:**
   - Read parenting/philosophy writers
   - Note essay structure

### **Swipe analysis:**

When you see a video/post you like:

**Ask 4 questions:**
1. **What's the hook?** (First 3 seconds)
   - Example: "Con tôi hỏi..." (story hook)
   - Learn: Start with concrete moment, not topic

2. **What's the structure?** (How content flows)
   - Example: Hook → Story → Lesson → CTA
   - Learn: Apply same structure to your content

3. **What's the angle?** (Why this angle, not another?)
   - Example: "Parenting perspective from work conflict" (unexpected angle)
   - Learn: Combine 2 areas for fresh angle

4. **What feeling did it trigger?** (Why I save/share?)
   - Example: "Recognition" (me too), "Learning" (actionable), "Inspiration"
   - Learn: Create content with clear emotion

### **Store swiped content:**

Use **2.3_CONTENT_SWIPE_FILE.md:**

```
## Hook Examples
- "Con tôi sợ..." (story opening)
- "Tôi từng tin..." (mindset opening)
- "3 cách..." (tips opening)

## Structures
- Story + Lesson (Tuyến 1)
- Problem + Solution (Tuyến 3)
- Belief + Shift (Tuyến 2)

## Angles
- Career insight → Parenting lesson
- Con's behavior → Life philosophy
- Failure → Learning
```

### **Example swipe:**

```
Found video: "Parenting coach shares why saying 'no' is self-care"

Hook: "Mẹ cảm thấy tội lỗi vì nói không với con?"
Structure: Hook → My struggle → Learning from X theory → How I changed → Result
Angle: Reframe "no" as love, not punishment
Feeling: Guilt relief + Validation

My idea: 
- Topic: Setting boundaries without guilt
- Hook: Similar opening
- Tuyến: 2 (Mindset) - reframe guilt
```

## CÁCH 4: PILLAR-BASED IDEATION (30 phút/tuần)

**Idea trigger:** Từ 4 pillars của brand (structured brainstorm)

### **The 4 Pillars (reminder):**

1. **Hành trình làm mẹ** (Parenting with learning)
2. **Vững vàng cảm xúc** (Emotional intelligence)
3. **Phát triển bản thân** (Self-growth)
4. **Thiết kế cuộc sống** (Life design)

### **Brainstorm process (Monday, 30 phút):**

**Step 1: Pick 1 pillar (7 phút)**
- Week 1: Pillar 1
- Week 2: Pillar 2
- Week 3: Pillar 3
- Week 4: Pillar 4
- Week 5: Cycle back to Pillar 1

**Step 2: Ask trigger questions (15 phút)**

**For Pillar 1 (Hành trình làm mẹ):**
- Con recently learned what?
- What confused con this week?
- When did I see con grow?
- What mistake did I make as mẹ?
- What did con teach me?

**For Pillar 2 (Vững vàng cảm xúc):**
- When did I struggle with emotions this week?
- How did I handle child's emotion?
- What emotion insight did I have?
- What old belief am I releasing?
- How did self-awareness help?

**For Pillar 3 (Phát triển bản thân):**
- What skill did I improve?
- What book/course am I learning?
- What habit changed?
- How did learning affect parenting?
- What's my next growth area?

**For Pillar 4 (Thiết kế cuộc sống):**
- How did I balance this week?
- What structure worked/didn't work?
- How did I protect "me time"?
- Work vs home: what changed?
- How did I design my day/week?

**Step 3: Write down all ideas (8 phút)**
- Don't filter, just write
- Add to 3.2_CONTENT_IDEAS_BANK.xlsx
- Mark pillar & tuyến

### **Example brainstorm (Pillar 1):**

```
Pillar: Hành trình làm mẹ

Q: Con learned what this week?
A: Con learned to acknowledge own feelings before reacting
   → Idea: Con teaching mẹ emotional awareness
   → Tuyến 1 (Story)

Q: What mistake did I make?
A: I interrupted con to "solve" problem, not listening
   → Idea: Listening vs fixing
   → Tuyến 2 (Mindset shift) or Tuyến 1 (Story)

Q: What confused con?
A: Why mẹ gets angry when con forgets things
   → Idea: Explaining mẹ's emotions to con
   → Tuyến 1 (Story) + Tuyến 3 (Tips)
```

## CÁCH 5: TREND + SEASONAL (10 phút/tuần)

**Idea trigger:** Xu hướng hiện tại, mùa, ngày lễ

### **Types of trends:**

1. **Seasonal:**
   - Back to school (August)
   - Holidays (Tết, Christmas, summer)
   - Seasons (weather changes, mood changes)

2. **Trending topics:**
   - Parenting news (new research, viral stories)
   - Social issues affecting families
   - Work trends (remote work, burnout)

3. **Calendar moments:**
   - Mother's Day (mẹ guilt content gold)
   - International Women's Day (phụ nữ content)
   - Mental Health Day
   - Back to school season

### **How to find trends:**

**TikTok trending sounds:**
- Check what's trending
- Adapt to your niche

**Google Trends:**
- Search "parenting" "self-growth" "work life balance"
- See what's trending

**News:**
- Parenting news in Vietnam
- Education changes

### **Angle with trends:**

**NOT:** "Here's what TikTok is doing"
**YES:** "How does [trend] relate to [audience pain point]?"

### **Example:**

```
Trend: Back to school anxiety content trending on TikTok
Your angle: "How to prepare emotionally for back to school"
   → Story: Con's anxiety before school
   → Solution: How you addressed it
   → Tuyến: 1 + 3
   → Timing: August-September

Holiday season:
Your angle: "Self-care during crazy family holidays"
   → Story: Struggling during holidays
   → Mindset: "Say no is also family time"
   → Tuyến: 2 (Mindset)
   → Timing: December
```
