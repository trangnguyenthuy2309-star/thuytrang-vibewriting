## KPI TARGETS - GIAI ĐOẠN 1 (Tháng 1-3)

### **Target Summary**

| Tháng | FB Followers | FB Engagement | TikTok Views | Comments/Engagement |
|-------|--------------|---------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Tháng 1** | 500-800 | 5-10% | 2K-5K | 100+ comments |
| **Tháng 2** | 1.5K-2K | 5-10% | 10K-15K | 300+ comments |
| **Tháng 3** | 3K-5K | 5-10% | 30K-50K | 800+ comments |

### **Success Indicators**

✅ **Healthy growth signs:**
- Followers growing week over week
- Comments showing real engagement (not spam)
- Share rate increasing (content is "reusable")
- Audience asking for more
- Similar comments from different people = resonance

⚠️ **Red flags (adjust if see):**
- Flat growth for 2+ weeks → Hook/format needs change
- Low comments/shares → Content not actionable enough
- Comments are just emojis → Tone not landing
- High reach, low engagement → Viral but not trusted
